## 🛠️ Project Overview
This project involved the technical architecture and deployment of a high-resolution 360° spatial tour for the American Youth Academy (AYA) in Tampa, FL. The goal was to bridge the gap between physical campus infrastructure and digital accessibility for remote enrollment.

## 🚀 Technical Highlights
* **Spatial Asset Integration:** Successfully bridged high-resolution 360° data into the school's CMS (Content Management System) architecture.
* **Google Ecosystem Optimization:** Synced virtual assets with Google Business Profiles to enhance local SEO and map-based discovery.
* **API-Based Outreach Consulting:** Designed a strategic framework for a WhatsApp Business API bridge, allowing the school to bypass low email open rates and deliver the tour directly to parent mobile devices.

## 📈 Business Impact
* **Remote Enrollment:** Enabled 100% digital "first-looks" for prospective families, reducing the friction of physical distance.
* **Engagement Growth:** Increased site "Time on Page" metrics through interactive media integration.
* **Demographic Alignment:** Solved communication gaps for non-email-centric demographics through mobile-first technical strategy.
